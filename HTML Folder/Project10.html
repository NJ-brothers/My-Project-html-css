IRONMAN

Ironman(2008)

"Iron Man" (2008) is the first film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, directed by Jon Favreau. It follows Tony Stark, a billionaire inventor played by Robert Downey Jr., who is captured by terrorists in Afghanistan. Instead of building a weapon for them, he creates a powerful suit of armor to escape. Back home, Stark perfects the suit and becomes Iron Man, using his technology to fight evil. The film blends action, humor, and stunning visual effects, with Downey's performance as Stark/Iron Man being a standout, setting the stage for the MCU's success.
