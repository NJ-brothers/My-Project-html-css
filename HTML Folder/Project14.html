Sahara

The Sahara is the world’s largest hot desert, spanning about 9.2 million square kilometers across North Africa. Known for its vast, barren landscapes, it is characterized by endless sand dunes, rocky plateaus, gravel plains, and occasional oases. Temperatures can soar above 50°C (122°F) during the day and drop significantly at night.
